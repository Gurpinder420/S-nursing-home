FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does Shaurya serve?
We currently serve Delhi NCR including Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Faridabad, Ghaziabad and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.
Are your nurses and caregivers certified?
Absolutely. All our nurses hold valid nursing licenses (GNM/B.Sc Nursing) and our caregivers are trained and certified. Every staff member undergoes background verification and health checks.
How quickly can you arrange a home nurse?
We can typically arrange a home nurse within 4–6 hours for standard requests. In emergency cases, we strive to respond within 2 hours. 24/7 support is available for urgent requirements.
What are your pricing plans?
Our pricing varies by service type, duration (hourly/daily/monthly) and care complexity. We offer transparent, competitive rates with no hidden fees. Contact us for a personalised quote.
Do you provide medical equipment on rent?
Yes, we supply and set up medical equipment including oxygen concentrators, hospital beds, wheelchairs, suction machines, BiPAP/CPAP devices and more — available on rental or purchase basis.
