Our Story

Born from a Passion to Serve

Shaurya Nursing Home & Home Care Services was founded in 2009 by Mr. Sushil Yadav with a single, powerful vision: to bring hospital-quality healthcare into the homes of patients who need it most.

What started as a small team of dedicated nurses and caregivers has grown into a comprehensive home healthcare organisation serving thousands of patients across Delhi NCR. Over the years, we have expanded our services, built a larger team of certified professionals, and invested in modern medical equipment — all while staying true to our founding principle of compassionate, personalised care.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable and high-quality home healthcare services that empower patients to heal and thrive in the comfort of their own homes — with dignity, respect and compassion at the centre of everything we do.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and recognised home healthcare provider in India — expanding our reach to underserved communities, leveraging technology to improve patient outcomes, and setting the highest standards for home-based medical care.
