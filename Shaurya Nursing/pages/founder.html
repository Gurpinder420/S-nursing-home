Biography

A Life Dedicated to Healing

Mr. Sushil Yadav is a visionary healthcare entrepreneur with over 15 years of deep experience in patient care and home healthcare services. Born and raised in Delhi, his journey into healthcare was inspired by a personal experience — watching his own family members struggle to access quality nursing care at home after hospitalisation.

Driven by this experience, Sushil founded Shaurya Nursing Home & Home Care Services in 2009 with a mission to bridge the gap between hospital discharge and full recovery at home. Under his leadership, the organisation has grown from a small nursing team to a comprehensive home healthcare provider serving thousands of patients across Delhi NCR.

A firm believer in combining professional expertise with genuine human compassion, Sushil has built a culture at Shaurya where every patient is treated with the same care and respect as a family member. His hands-on approach, attention to quality and dedication to continuous improvement have made Shaurya one of the most trusted names in home healthcare in the region.

Leadership Philosophy

Care First. Always.

"I believe that every patient — regardless of age, background or condition — deserves dignity, comfort and the very best medical care. Our job is not just to treat illness; it is to restore hope, independence and quality of life. That is what drives me and our entire team every single day."

— Sushil Yadav, Founder
